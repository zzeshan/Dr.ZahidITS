About

I am an Assistant Professor at Prince Sultan University, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and Team Leader of the Intelligent Transportation Systems Section at the Robotics and Internet-of-Things Laboratory (RIOTU). My work combines advanced networking, intelligent mobility, secure communication, and scalable algorithmic design for connected systems.

I hold a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Southwest Jiaotong University, China. My academic work spans intelligent transportation systems, vehicular ad hoc networks, routing and clustering, IoT, cybersecurity, SDN-enabled mobility, and quantum-inspired optimization for next-generation communication and transportation networks.
