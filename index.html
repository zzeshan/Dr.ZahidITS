Dr. Zahid Khan

Assistant Professor at Prince Sultan University, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and Team Leader of the Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) Section at the Robotics and Internet-of-Things Laboratory (RIOTU).

Research interests include advanced computing systems and networks, intelligent transportation systems, vehicular ad hoc networks (VANETs), routing and clustering optimization, cybersecurity, IoT, and quantum-inspired intelligent mobility.
